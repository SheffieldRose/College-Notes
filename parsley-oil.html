Parsley oil

To make the oil, I first blanched some fresh parsley in boiling water and plunged into icey water to fix the colour before pureeing in a blender with olive oil. I then passed this liquid through a muslin, cooled in the blast chiller and skimmed the frozen water from the flavoured oil and bottled.
