Bouchées ratatouilles

For the bouchées we cut circles of puff pastry and baked with an egg wash before filling with a ratatouille that we made from frying off shallots and garlic, then adding courgette, aubergine, green pepper, and tomatoes, all cut to a macedoine and running chiffonade basil through it.

While all still hot, we topped with slices of black olive.
