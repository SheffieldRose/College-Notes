Oysters Rockefeller

This is an example of one of the dishes that we prepared ourselves during this demonstration day.

  1. First we shucked the oysters
  2. Fried smoked bacon until crispy and chopped into small pieces, then placed onthe bottom of the shell
  3. Made a florentine from butter, oil, salt and spinach and placed on top of the bacon
  4. Put oysters on top of spinach
  5. Put chopped spring onions on top of oysters
  6. Piped a compound garlic butter on top of oysters
  7. Put cheddar on top
  8. Put parmesan, parmesan, salt, thyme, breadcrumbs on top
  9. More fried bacon
  10. Then we gratinated this all under the grill until bubbling and nicely grilled
