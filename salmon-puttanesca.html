Hot smoked salmon puttanesca

Debeard mussels and drop into pan frying shallots and add a splash of white wine and a splash of fish stock.

We hot smoked the salmon over burning oak chips after coating it with a herb-crust and pink peppercorns, both stuck on with honey.

  • Olive oil
  • Shallots
  • Capers
  • Black olives
  • Anchovies
  • Basil
  • ½ tomato concasse
  • Lemon zest
  • Garlic
  • Chilli
  • White wine
  • Serve with linguine
