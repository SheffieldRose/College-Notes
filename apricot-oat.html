Apricot & oat bread

To make this, we started by mixing 800g strong white flour, 200g wholemeal flours, 400g water, 200g sourdough, 100g oats and left to sit while we chopped 200g apricots.

We then added 40g salt and 60g yeast, mixed for 3 minutes slow, then 25 minutes fast and split into two rounds and proved for an hour before baking first at 250°c, then 220°c for a total of 25 minutes.
