Béchamel sauce

The béchamel that we made was made with a white roux by cooking out some plain flour in melted butter keeping the white appearance of the mix and bulking out with milk to get a smooth and medium-thick sauce, enough for 4 people.

The lasagne we made in the pasta unit called for a plain béchamel sauce.
