Darne de merlu trapiste

Taking a darne from the hake in the same way as described for the saumon Chambord, I tied using food-safe string around the edge of the fish to help hold its shape during the cooking process.

I pan-fried the hake in butter while making the sauce (meanwhile, deep frying rings of sliced onion).

The sauce I made from fish stock, stock browning (made from caramelising and darkening sugar in a pan to achieve a dark-brown, meaty colour), and Leffe Brun Belgian beer and reducing slightly.

I served all this together with an anchovy compound butter.
