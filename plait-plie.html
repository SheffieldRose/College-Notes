Plait de plie en tresse

Using the longest fillet, we cut lengthways into three strips for plaiting, coated in butter and stored in the fridge while we prepared the rest of the dish.

We served the fish with a trimmed and peeled, pan-fried portobello mushroom which we stuffed with a mushroom duxelles, and topped with a pan-fried scallop.

We then grilled the plait de plie under the salamander and placed on top of the scallop with deep fried leek “hair”.

To finish we put a napé of sauce dugléré around the fish.
