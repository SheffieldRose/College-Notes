Sauce Dugléré

To make this, I reduced a fish stock with white wine then made a velouté as described under “velouté” above, added some fish glace, and monté-d the sauce au beurre, then finished with a tomato concasse and finely chopped parsley.

We served this with the plait de plie en tresse
