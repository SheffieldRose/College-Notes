Coquilles saint Jacques à la parisienne

To make this, first shuck the scallops and spray the shell with release spray to ensure easy release on service. Boil and mash potatoes then pipe onto the shell, lightly sprayed with release spray.

Make a sauce from finely chopped fried shallots, white wine and fish glace and reduce by half.

Finish the sauce off with cream. Pan-fry the scallops and caviar separately so they are cooked but remain tender then place on the shell and grill under the salamander to give the scallops a golden-brown colour. Garnish with sauce and cibles of chive.
