Pintade glacée au citrons verts

We split the guineau fowl straight down the middle, ensuring that the halved carcass was neatly trimmed, seasoned and pan-fried bone-side down.

Once we’d done this, we roasted in the oven and then drizzled with honey. With this resting, we caramelised some lime zest to serve with the dish while frying some beansprouts, courgette and glazed carrots.

Setting aside and keeping warm, we then deglazed the pan with soy, lime juice, sherry and a demi-glace. We served all of this together on a platter.
