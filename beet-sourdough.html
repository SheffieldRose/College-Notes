Beetroot sourdough

To make this, we mixed, 1kg of strong white flour, 20g salt, 320g fresh grated beetroot, 20g olive oil, 440g sourdough starter, 400ml water and rested it for 10 minutes.

We folded this, rested for 10 minutes then folded again. We fermented this for 1 hour, divided into 520g piece rounds, rested it for another 10 minutes and put into loaf tins until risen then baked at 240°c with steam and 220°c once the bread went into the dry oven.
