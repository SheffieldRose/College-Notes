Sweet and sour fried chicken with noodles

We started by making a pasta mix, resting, rolling and cutting, then setting aside ready for cooking.

The sauce came next and we made a sugar gastric with malt vinegar, sugar and pineapple juice and reducing. We then added ketchup and soy sauce.

For the vegetables, we cut a brunoir of red pepper, allumette carrots, a brunoir of ginger, macedoine pineapple which we put into the sauce.

Next we coated chunks of chicken in cornflour and potato starch and deep fried until golden.

Meanwhile, we prepared the garnishes which were, sugar snap peas halved lengthways, toasted sesame oil, bean srpouts, green chilli, blanched pak choi and spring onion.

Once the chicken was done, we cooked the pasta in boiling water quickly and served all together.
