Chicken saltimbocca with orzo

To make the saltimbocca we raised the supreme, butterflied the cut, stuffed it with sage leaves and wrapped in parma ham with sage leaves sandwiched in between.

Then we pan-fried this in foaming butter and basted before finishing off in the oven.

We served this with an orzo pasta side through which we folded chive, butter, sliced black olives, concasse, capers, thyme and spinach.

Then we cut the bottoms off some blow-torched tomatoes and put them into the chicken pan to warm through. Once done, we served them all together.
