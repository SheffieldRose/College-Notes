Canapé Ritchie

For the canapé Ritchie, we first use cutting forms to trim thick slices of cucumber into uniform shapes and cut them all to the same length. We scooped out a hollow space into which we would later put our filling and blanched the cucumber pieces, then set them aside.

For the filling we started with a béchamel into which we cooked pieces of smoked haddock that we flaked as the sauce cooked, making sure that the sauce and fish was evenly combined. We finished the sauce with gruyè and chopped parsley.

Once the filling had chilled, we spooned it into the cucumber cups and served with a cooked crayfish tail to garnish.
