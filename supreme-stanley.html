Shallow-fried chicken suprême Stanley with sherried curry sauce

Starting with the chicken breast, we coated it in a seasoned curry powder and flour mix and shallow-fried it in foaming butter in a pan, then set aside while we made the sauce.

Deglazing the same pan with shallots, chicken stock and sweet sherry, we made the sauce by adding cream to thicken it as well as monter-ing au beurre and vaner-ing.

We finished this off with some batons of sliced mushrooms.
