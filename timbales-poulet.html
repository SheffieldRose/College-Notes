Timbales de poulet aux champignons à la crème

Breaking down a chicken, we took the breast and pureed with an egg and chives to make a forcemeat with salt, to tighten the proteins.

Adding salt to a bowl of ice to keep the mousse cool, we then placed the chicken bowl on top and slowly beating cream into the chicken to emulsify.

This we did until it starts to leave the sides of the bowl. We passed this so that it was texturally consistent, then we piped this into some timbales and stuffed by piping a previously made duxelles into the middle of the mousse.

We then baked in the steam oven, while making a sauce madeira, and garnished with blanched asparagus, trimmed leeks and fried mushrooms.
