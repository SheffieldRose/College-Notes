Health, Safety & Admin

This section pertains to best practices in the kitchen, which we have carried out each lesson by taking strict hygiene measures and food safety precautions. Examples include:

  • Great personal hygiene, showering before and after working with food, washing hands regularly and between handling different foodstuffs to avoid cross-contamination between dishes.

  • Storing food at a safe temperature, in freezers at -18°c and fridges set at 1-5°c.

  • Cooking to the appropriate temperatures to kill any bacteria that may be present.
