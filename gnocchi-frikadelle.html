Gnocchi with veal frikadelle and gremolata

We made the gnocchi from potato flour, parmesan cheese, egg yolk, and some water which we rolled cut into portions and pan-fried. In another pan, we fried off meatballs made from ethically reared veal mince, salt, breadcrumbs moistened with milk, chopped sage, onion, parmesan, chopped parsley, and a pinch of nutmeg. Once these were nicely fried, we placed them on top of a cold red pepper coulis and gremolata made from lemon, garlic and parsley with a parmesan crisp that we had made by grilling grated parmesan on a baking sheet.
