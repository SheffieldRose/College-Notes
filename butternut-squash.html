Stuffed butternut squash

For the stuffed butternut squash, we first use cutting forms to trim thick slices of butternut squash into uniform shapes and cut them all to the same length.

We scooped out a hollow space into which we would later put our filling and cooked the butternut squash pieces in boiling water, then set them aside.

To make the filling, we cut a large bruoir of butternut squash and fried it with finely diced onion and caraway seeds. To bind it, we stirred cream into the pan and reduced until the the ingredients were well combined.

We then spooned the mix into the butternut cups and garnished with two fronds of chive.
