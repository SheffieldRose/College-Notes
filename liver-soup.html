German-style liver soup with potato dumplings

To make the German-style liver soup, we first made some gnocchi which we learned how to make during our pasta unit from potato flour, parmesan cheese, egg yolk, and some water. This, we rolled and left to set in the fridge until use.
To make the soup, we started with a tomato demi-glace and two tablespoons of tomato puree in a pan. Then we matched the volume with a German wheat beer and brought the pan to simmer. To garnish, we made a tomato concasse and set this aside until service. We also cooked some carrots in the soup while we pan-fried the liver and gnocchi. Once this was done, we added all the ingredients to the soup pan and served in a soup turine.
