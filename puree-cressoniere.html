Purée cressionière

To make this soup we first boiled potatoes until they were tender and fried off some onion.

Then we blanched a bag of watercress and plunged it into ice to fix the chlorophyll to help maintain the soup's vibrant green colour and pureed all of this together, loosened with vegetable stock to make a fully vegetarian dish then served in a soup turine.
