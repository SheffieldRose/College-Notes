Goujons de plie Murat

To make the goujons, we sliced a fillet of plaice (filleted from whole as described above) into smaller pieces then followed these steps:

  • Coat goujons in flour and fry in pan
  • Pan-fry artichoke for a bit of colour and add a squeeze of lemon
  • Pan-fry macedoine potatoes and add a meat glaze
  • Pan-fried diced artichoke
  • Garnish with chives, tomato concasse, capers, beurre noisette made from a compound anchovy butter, celery leaf and deep-fried shallot onion rings.
