Truite au court bouillon avec sauce divine

Court bouillon for deep poaching • 2 leeks • 2 sticks celery • 1 big carrot • White peppercorn • 25g salt • 1 shallot • 3 bay leaves • Big sprig thyme • 200ml White wine vinegar • 500ml water • Serve with traditional garnish for court bouillon – poached cucumber, turned carrots and new potatoes. Trout is traditionally served with head and tail on. Sauce divine Hollandaise with nut brown butter and a drop of sherry – hollandaise as explained in the hot sauces section.
