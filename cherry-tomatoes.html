Cherry tomatoes stuffed with cream cheese and olive puree

For the cherry tomatoes, we shaved a small amount off the bottom to make sure that they stood flat and didn't roll around.

We then cut the tops off and scooped out the seeds to make room for the filling.

We then quickly blanched the tomatoes so that they were firm yet tender to the bite.

To fill them, we mixed goat's cheese with double cream to give a smooth texture and piped it into the tomato.

To serve, we placed pesto made with basil oil in the bottom of a Chinese soup spoon and the put the cherry tomatoes on top. To garnish we put a dot of olive puree and cibles of chive on top with the tomato "lids", stood up vertically in the cream.
