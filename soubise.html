Soubise with œufs en cocotte

To make this dish, we fried lots of onion in butter with picked thyme until transluscent, added chicken stock and blitzed in a blender until completely smooth, passing it through a chinois back into the pan and reducing by half. We then added equal parts cream and boiled. We poured this into a ramekin which held a whole egg and placed under the salamander to poach the eggs in the onion cream sauce and finished with a disc of toast and a drizzle of balsamic reduction.
