Poussin gruyère en spatchcock

After removing the wishbone and wing tips, we cut the poussin chicken along the spine and flattened out, removing the breastbone.

Then we trussed the leg bones through the flesh of the bird, using its own structure to secure the meat so it cooked neatly and evenly.

Then we slow-grilled the poussin and marinated in honey and mustard half-way through to avoid burning the marinade.

We finished this part of the dish with a chapleur and garnished with sauce Robert, watercress and pommes paille.
