Sauerkraut

Using a non-traditional quick recipe, we made sauerkraut by frying finely shredded white cabbage, sliced onion, crushed garlic and caraway seeds in duck fat in a large pan to soften and then added a splash of white wine.

To finish, we garnished this we added half a teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda, some glazed onions, glazed turned carrots, steamed turned potatoes and a handful of chopped parsley.

To garnish I topped the sauerkraut with smoked sausage, garlic sausage and braised ham hock.
