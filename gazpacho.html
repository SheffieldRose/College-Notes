Gazpacho andalouse

This is a cold soup, the ingredients of which we placed into a pan and marinated before blitzing in a blender and passing.

During the marination time, we chopped the garnish into macedoine pieces.

The ingredients we marinated in a bowl in the fridge were:

  • Baguette
  • Plum tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 50 ml red wine vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Passata
  • Red pepper
  • Red onion
  • Cucumber
  • Cumin
  • Marjoram
  • Basil
  • Salt and pepper

The ingredients that we chopped into a macedoine cut were, tomatoes (concasse), cucumber, pepper, black olives, red onion.

This I topped with croutons and an herb oil, made from blanching fresh herbs, then plunging into ice to arrest the oxidisation process caused by the enzyme polyphenol oxidase being exposed to and reacting with oxygen in the air.

Blanching the herbs quickly breaks down the polyphenol oxidase and prevents browning once the herbs are chopped.

After taking the herbs out of the ice, we put them into a liquidiser with some olive oil and puréed.

I then passed out the solids through a muslin and was left with a green, herb flavoured oil.
