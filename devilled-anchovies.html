Devilled deep fried anchovies

Cayenne pepper/paprika/white pepper/salt – mix into flour to make a “francaise” batter.

Defrost anchovies in milk and then panez the anchovies in this mix then deep-fry.

Lemon in muslin is how we garnished this fried fish.
