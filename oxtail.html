Oxtail soup

This was made by frying off a mirepoix and mixing in 1 tbsp plain flour and tomato puree as well as 300ml red wine, 1.5 litres of brown beef stock and adding the oxtail, which has been sliced cleanly through the joint.

We cooked this down until the meat on the bone was tender and removed the flesh, discarding the bone and placing the meat back into the pan.
