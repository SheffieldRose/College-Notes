Eviscerating a bird

To eviscerate a bird starting from scratch (for example a game bird such as the pheasant we prepared when exploring game), start by plucking the feathers and washing the bird.

Remove the neck and placed hand inside the carcass on the organs, rotating them and sweeping against the outside of the cavity to ensure all is gathered, then pull out.

Remove the feet at the knuckle and trim off any sinew or cartilage.
