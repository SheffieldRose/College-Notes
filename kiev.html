Deep-fried chicken supreme “Kiev”, sauce diable

To make this, we took the supreme of the chicken, stuffed with a compound garlic butter then panéd and breadcrumbed.

We then deep-fried the chicken and finished it off in the oven. This we served on top of braised, stock flavoured rice and sauce diable.
