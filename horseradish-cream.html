Radish and horseradish cream

For the radishes, we first use cutting forms to trim thick slices of mooli radish into uniform shapes and cut them all to the same length. We scooped out a hollow space into which we would later put our filling and blanched the radish pieces, then set them aside.

For the filling, we whipped double cream to a thick consistency, ensuring it didn't split and pureed a pre-made horseradish sauce to a smooth consistency.

Once done, we stirred the two together and piped the mixture into the radish cups through a fluted nozzle and garnished with a sprinkle of cayenne pepper, blanched asparagus and celery leaf.
