Ravioli courge musqué

To make the filling, we made a mushroom duxelles base and combined with grated cheese and brunoir butternut squash.

This, we fried all together and set to rest in the fridge while we made the pasta. Again, we put the pasta mix into the fridge to rest while we prepared the garnishes.

To garnish, we used strips of bacon, tuiled and baked cheese, macedoine butternut squash and chopped chives.

Once this was ready we rolled, cut and filled the pasta with the previously prepared ravioli filling. We dropped this into salted, water with a drop of olive oil to prevent the pasta sticking together and cooked until the pasta was ready and hot throughout.

We then put the garnishes on top and finished with a poached egg and sage butter with lemon zest.
