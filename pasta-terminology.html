Notes

Two ingredients commonly paired with pasta on a general day-to-day basis are cream and mushrooms:

A note on cream -

  • Single cream – 15-17% fat
  • Whipping cream – 38 % fat
  • Double cream – 48% fat
  • Clotted cream – 70% fat

A note on mushrooms –

  • Black truffles grow on the leaf mould from beech and oak trees
  • A boleat is a fungus with pores, not gills. They are not mushrooms – cepes/porcinis are an example of this
  • Girolles – a honey citrus flavour
  • Honey fungus is another term for shamigi mushrooms
  • Morels are hugely flavoursome and highly prized as a result
