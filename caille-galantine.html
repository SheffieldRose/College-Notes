Caille galantine

To do this, we boned the quail out from the inside and removed the thigh bones and carcass. This we stuffed with a chicken mousse, seasoned and flavoured with nutmeg, a pistachio puree and parsley.

We then trussed and wrapped the bird in clingfilm into a galantine shape and put into a cold pan of water to make sure that it heated up at the same time as the water and didn’t overcook.

In the meantime, we made a gastric from madeira, sugar, malt vinegar and prepared a garnish to put in the sauce of sultanas, orange zest, peeled grapes, parsley tomato concasse and blanched and peeled pistachios.

Once cooked we fried some colour onto the quail, glazing with honey under the grill.

We finished the whole dish by garnishing with orange segments and a little oil on the sliced bird to lift presentation.
