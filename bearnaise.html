Béarnaise sauce

For the béarnaise sauce, we began with a white wine reduction that included shallots, tarragon, white peppercorns and tarragon vinegar which we emulsified with egg yolks and bulked out with melted butter to make enough for around 4 portions.

Once done we ran through with some chopped chives and tarragon.
