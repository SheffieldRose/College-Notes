Sausage tortelli with spinach

To make this, we rolled flat sheets of pasta that we stuffed with cooked Italian sausage meat and cooked quickly in boiling water while preparing the garnishes.

We served the sausage tortelli with blanched spinach, basil oil (that we made by blanching basil, pureeing it with olive oil and passing it through a muslin, freezing then skimming ice from oil) and tomato concasse.
