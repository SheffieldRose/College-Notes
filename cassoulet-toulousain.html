Cassoulet toulousain

To begin, we braised down cubes of paprika sausage, garlic sausage, and slices of bacon in some chopped tomato, garlic and onion.

In another pan we caramelised two finely chopped onions and added four cloves of finely chopped garlic and fried rillons of pork breast.

We then took a matignon bordelaise (which comprises carrot, celery, leek, half an onion, a sprig of thyme, and a bay leaf), fried it and added chicken stock and 300ml of haricot beans in water and stewed for four hours with the meat added in.

We finished this with a tomato concasse and gratinated it with breadcrumbs on top at the very end to serve.

This dish was made with and served alongside the duck confit referred to under duck confit in the game section of this document.
