Dressed crab

  1. Boil crab for 12 minutes per kilo then chill to ease the removal of meat from shell
  2. Hard boil eggs and plunge into cold water
  3. Separate the boiled eggs and grate the whites, then grate the yolks
  4. Break crab apart and separate white meat from brown
  5. Mash brown meat with lemon zest, cibles of chive, white pepper, Worcestershire sauce and fresh white breadcrumbs
  6. Dress crab by placing the individual components back inside a cleaned shell in a neat pattern with some cibles of chive and strained tinned anchovies to garnish
  7. Serve as an hors d'œurve
