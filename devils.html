Devils on horseback

Ensuring clean work surfaces, and that we were using a red coloured board, we worked in teams and divided the making of this dish amongst ourselves.

Taking turns, we tightly rolled prunes in thinly sliced bacon, in two rotations. To keep it together we pinned it with a cocktail stick before cooking in the oven until the bacon was cooked through.

Once we had done around five in a pair, we rotated to work on a different canapé.

At service, we served this with a mango chutney and a fine brunoir of chilli.
