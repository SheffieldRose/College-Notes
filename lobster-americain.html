Lobster americain

After butchering the lobster and removing the flesh as intact as possible, the tail was pan-fried. At the same time the sauce was made from a mirepoix, onion, tomato puree, thyme, cayenne pepper, butter and brandy and flambéed.

Then the brown meat was added to this along with a sauce Nantua and served. Traditionally, rice would also be served.
