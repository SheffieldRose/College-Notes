Filleting a plaice

First, to make sure the plaice was fresh I checked that it had bright, red gills and bright unsunken eyes, then followed the steps below:

  1. Remove the skirt using a filleting knife or scissors
  2. Make an incision behind the back of the head
  3. Cut down the lateral line of the fish onto ribs
  4. Cut across tail
  5. Going back to the lateral incision and using you filleting knife between the flesh and the ribs, sweep down and away from the spine in long strokes to maintain integrity of the fish
  6. Taking the detached fillet, pinch tail end with salt in order to be able to hold the fish firmly while skinning
  7. Cut down through flesh to skin and then pull tight against knife and blue fish chopping board to remove the rest of the skin all the way down the fillet.
  8. Flip fish around and repeat the same actions using the weight of the fillet to help it fall away from the bone and show where to slice with knife.
