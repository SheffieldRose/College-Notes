Tartelette avec paté

For the tartelette, we used 3 layers of filo pastry each brushed with butter, baked in tart cases at 180°c for 6 minutes.

For the filling, we extended a paté made from puréed liver with pomade butter to make a "foie gras" type mix, which we then piped through a fluted nozzle into the tart cases and served it cold with a chopped parsley garnish.
