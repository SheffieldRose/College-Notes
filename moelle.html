Canapé Moëlle avec champignons

For this we grilled circular slices of bread then topped the sippets with beef bone marrow and grilled again.
For the mushrooms, we fried sliced mushrooms quickly and combined with double cream to create a sauce to bind them then placed them on top of the sippets.
