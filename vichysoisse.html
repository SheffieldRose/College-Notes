Vichysoisse

This is another chilled soup that is made from a white mirepoix with leeks, maris piper potatoes and chopped chives as a garnish.

We first sweated off the vegetables under a cartouche of clingfilm around the top of the pan and then filled the pan to the top with water for the soup to thicken out naturally as the potatoes blended with the water.

Once cooked, we liquidised the soup and chilled. Once chilled, we served this with cibles of chive on top.
