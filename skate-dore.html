Skate d’oré de sauce grenoblaise

Simply done, the cut of skate wing which is traditionally supplied as such was lightly floured and pan-fried.

Once I’d done this, I sprinkled it with finely chopped parsley and lemon juice then drizzled with a beurre noisette which contains capers and served with a lemon wedge.
