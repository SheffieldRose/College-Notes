Escalope de dinde hongroise

An escalope is where you take the loin meat or breast of poultry and hammer it flat.

We did this by first cutting an escalope piece from the breast of a turkey, removing the silver, hammering and flouring with paprika and salt as well.

We then shallow fried this in butter and oil.

Placing aside, we made the sauce by frying mushrooms with paprika, adding cream and demi-glace, then put the escalope back into the sauce and served.
