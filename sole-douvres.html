Sole de douvres colbert – DEMO ONLY

This was only demonstrated by our course leader, we didn't do this.

* Dover sole skin is very rough which explains the less-than-delicate method of skinning

To fillet :

  • Remove skirt
  • Cut through skin onto tailbone and scrape back skin so there is enough to grip
  • Pull/peel skin off fish
  • Repeat on underside
  • This is then breaded and fried/grilled whole and served with an infused, melted butter/chopped parsley mix and deep-fried parsley to garnish.
