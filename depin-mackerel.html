Depinning a mackerel

To do this follow the steps below:

  1. Remove the head and pull the guts out
  2. Snip fins from the underside of the fish
  3. Scale the fish and rinse off any scales, then pat dry
  4. Slice the belly from top to tail
  5. Remove any remaining guts.
  6. Flatten the fish by pressing your thumb firmly down on the spine with the ribs placed flat on either side
  7. Use a knife to nick under the spine and tease the spine and ribcage from the flesh all the way down, using a filleting knife to help with any tricky parts and discard the bones.
  8. Using fish tweezers, gently pull any remaining bones from the flesh, pressing lightly on it so as not to mash the meat.
  9. Check fish is free from bones by running hand along flesh
