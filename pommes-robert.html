Pommes Robert

For this, we coated a baking potato in oil and a pinch of salt and baked in the oven.

Once cool enough to handle we removed the filling and placed the skins back in the baking tray, flattening at the bottom and put back in the oven to crisp up.

In the meantime, we mashed the flesh of the potato and combined with cibles of chive, egg yolk, emmental cheese and parmesan cheese to make a smooth filling for the potato skin.

Once we had put the filling back into the skin , we put some grated emmental cheese on top and gratinated under the salamander grill.
