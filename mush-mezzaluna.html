Mushroom mezzaluna

To make this, half-moon shaped pasta, we first made the pasta with a sachet of saffron then set to rest in the fridge whilst we prepared the mushroom filling.

We then rolled and cut the pasta into spheres, filled with the cooked mushroom mix and folded into a crescent shape.

We then cooked the mezzaluna and finished with a garnish of chopped and cooked mushroom, pine nuts, parsley, anchovy, parmesan and sundried tomatoes.
