Ravioli marinara

To make the ravioli, we rolled flat sheets of pasta which we then stuffed with a mix of Italian sausage and set aside in the fridge until we were ready to use them.

We made the sauce using olive oil, onion, garlic, bay leaf, oregano, white wine, tomato passata and a pinch of nutmeg.

Once this was ready, we quickly cooked the pasta in boiling water until tender, drained and folded through the marinara sauce.
