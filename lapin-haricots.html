Lapin aux haricots

  • Coat rabbit pieces in flour and fry off.
  • Fry off Matignon and add rabbit
  • Add haricot beans and white wine to cover
  • Chicken stock to braise
  • Put lentils in
  • Finish off with a tomato concasse to garnish.
