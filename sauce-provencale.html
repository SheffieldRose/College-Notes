Sauce provençale

To begin with, we fried off these ingredients:

  • 25ml olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ½ shallot

Then we added these ingredients and cooked down until flavours had combined and matured:

  • Bay leaf
  • Thyme
  • 25ml white wine vinegar

Tomato concasse

  • 150ml passata
  • Parsley to garnish
