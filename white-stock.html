White stock

To make a white stock, using chicken bones, you start by washing the bones, or blanching them.

Place into a fresh pan of water, then bring to the boil and skim.

Then replace the water, boil and leave to simmer for around 2 hours, skimming it regularly until you have a clear white stock, flavoured by the chicken.
