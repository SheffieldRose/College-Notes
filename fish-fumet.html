Fish stock and fumet de poisson

For a fumet (similar to a meat glace, but made with fish), it is simply a fish stock reduced by half.

To make both, we buttered an entire pan to avoid sticking and filled it with onion, a bay leaf, 4 peppercorns, parsley stalks, lemon, fish bones (no eyes or gills - these make it grey) to sweat down with colouring and then added 100ml white wine and 2 parts water.

If making a fumet or a jellified stock, monkfish is an appropriate choice as they are full of gelatine.
