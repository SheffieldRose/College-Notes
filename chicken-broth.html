Chicken broth

As broth always contains a pulse of some kind, we used pearl barley to add some rustic texture to our dish. We started with a chicken bouillon (fonds de volaille), which consisted of a white chicken stock made from a washed chicken carcass, thyme, peppercorns, a bay leaf, a carrot, half a leek, an onion and water covering those ingredients.

We finished the dish with the shredded meat of the chicken from which we had used the carcass, brunoir celery, brunoir swede, brunoir carrot, paysanne white and green of leek, and cooked pearl barley after having cooked the chicken in the oven and quickly cooking the vegetables in the stock.
