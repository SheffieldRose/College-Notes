Tomato consommé Bloody Marys

This is an example of our vegetarian consomme, which we made using aquafarbe (chickpea water used to replace eggs in vegan recipes - and something that can be used alone to provide the protein in a fully vegan consommé) and eggs to make a light and fruity consommé, perfect for using in a Bloody Mary canapé.

With the protein "raft" made from these two ingredients, we added it to the liquid components of this dish which were passata and Worcestershire sauce and seasoned it with cayenne pepper and a little salt and sugar.

We simmered this until the raft had gathered any impurities in the soup and made sure that the liquid was clear. Removing the raft, we passed the consommé through a coffee filter to ensure any left-over scum would be collected.

We then chilled this in the blast chiller until it was serving temperature and added cold vodka and served in shot glasses with celery tips to garnish.
