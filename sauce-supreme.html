Sauce suprême

This we made by adapting a velouté. To the velouté as described above, we added mushrooms that had been cooked in a dash of cream and passed through a chinois, retaining some to go into the velouté sauce and some to be removed and chopped down into a duxelles, used for stuffing vegetables later on.
