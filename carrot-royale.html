Carrot royale

Carrot royale is a type of steamed vegetable timbale. To start with, we chopped the carrots into smaller pieces and cooked through in a pan with butter.

After this, we pureed the carrots in a blender and seasoned.

We loosened this up with cream and bound it with an egg. In order to add a bit of stability, although not necessary, we added a spoonful of bechamel sauce before steaming in a buttered ramekin inside a steam oven.

The bechamel sauce is made with flour cooked out in an equal part of butter. This is bulked out and brought to an appropriate sauce thickness with whole milk.
