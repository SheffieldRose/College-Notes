Maquereaux à la provençale

To make this, we filleted the fish as described in the method section and rolled the fillets. We kept their shape by trussing the roll together with a skewer.

Sauce provencal

  • 25ml olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ½ shallot
  • Bay leaf
  • Thyme
  • 25ml white wine vinegar
  • 150ml passata
  • Tomato concasse and loads of parsley to garnish
