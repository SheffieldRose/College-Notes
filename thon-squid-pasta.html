Thon pavé with squid ink pasta

For the squid ink pasta, we mixed 100g 00 flour, 1 egg yolk, 30g cuttlefish sepia which we rested, then rolled and cut into noodles on the pasta roller.

For the mussels, we made a broth using the liquid from the shells after steaming the mussels in their own juices, white wine, finely diced shallots, fish glace and saffron which we reduced. To this we added mussel meat, double cream and leeks.

In another pan, we fried shamigi mushrooms and removed from the heat, placing blanched cherry tomatoes and asparagus in too to glaze with the oil.

We then quickly cooked the pasta, while searing the tuna pavé and plated all together with a tomato concasse garnish.
