Spatchcocking

To spatchcock a bird, prepare as you would a whole bird by removing wing tips and feet. Trim the bone that is left to tidy it up. Remove the spine by cutting and open then press flat. Remove the ribs that remain by slicing them off and make an incision in the flesh on each side through which you can truss the bird with thigh bone to ensure that the bird stays flat and keeps its shape while grilling. Use skewers through the bird in an “X” shape to hold the bird over the flame.
