Red pepper gastric

We made the gastric by melting down equal parts vinegar and sugar with one chopped clove of garlic. We then blanched a tomato to loosen the skin and peeled it. We then removed the seeds and chopped the flesh down into fine cubes to make a tomato concasse. Then we blackened a red pepper over an open flame and ran under cold water to remove the skin and seeds. We added what remained to the sugar/vinegar gastric and blitzed in a blender until smooth. Once this was done, we ran the concasse through the sauce to add texture.
