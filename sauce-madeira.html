Sauce Madeira

To make the sauce madeira, we sweated down some very finely chopped shallots in 100ml of madeira wine and added demi-glace, reducing until syrupy and coating the back of a spoon. We then adjusted the texture, thickness and appearance by adding 50ml of double cream to finish the sauce in line with traditional recipe.
