Pavé de thon grillé et sauce Choron

  • Sprinkle one side with cornflour and scorch grill marks into tuna and then chargrill
  • Dress fresh rocket with salt and olive oil
  • Tomato skin shaped as a rose
  • Peel lemon with a knife and desegment NO PEEL OR SKIN OR PITH
  • Add capers to pan

Sauce choron - the sauce choron in this recipe is a warm butter emulsion – for comparison reasons, it’s like a béarnaise sauce with tomato concasse in it. The reduction is:

  • Shallots
  • White wine vinegar
  • White peppercorn
  • 1 bayleaf
  • Tarragon

Pass through a chinois and make into a sabayon. Add chopped chives, chopped tarragon, tomato concasse and tomato paste.
