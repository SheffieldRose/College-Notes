Focaccia

This recipe started by placing all the dry ingedients (500g strong white flour, 8g salt, 10g dried yeast) into a mixer and adding 350g water and mixing for 3 minutes on a slow speed and 6 minutes on a fast speed.

Once done, we placed the wet dough onto a baking sheet, brushed it with olive oil, sprinkled it with chopped garlic and proved for 40 minutes.

When proved, we made indentations with our fingers in the dough and baked for 25 minutes at 235°c. Once done, we topped it with lightly fried chopped rosemary
