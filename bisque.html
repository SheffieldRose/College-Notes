Bisque de crevettes

To make this, we first fried of a mirepoix of carrot, onion, garlic and leek.

Into this we put 300g of shell on prawns, 1tbsp tomato purée, a splash of dry wine wine, a splash of brandy, fish stock that we had made earlier that day, and short grain rice as a thickening agent.

Once the brandy had flambéed and the rice fully cooked, we put the whole mixture into a blender and puréed until completely smooth, passing through a muslin placed over a conical strainer to catch any potential pieces that had been missed by the machine.

To serve, we swirled sour cream on top with cayenne pepper.
