Hot smoked mussels with fennel

Start by lighting setting a pan on top of a flame and fill the bottom with food-safe smoking chips.

Once smouldering, place a rack above the chips so that food may be smoked while covered in the pan.

Place the mussels onto this rack with fennel seeds and cover.

Check the pan after a couple of minutes and remove once the shells have opened.
