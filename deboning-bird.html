Deboning a bird (cocklet or poussin)

  1. Pasron's nose off
  2. Wishbone out
  3. Take knuckles/wing tips/winglets off
  4. Remove neck
  5. Where neck was, cut around the carcass, between bone and flesh
  6. Use hands to tease meat from the bone to loosen
  7. Snap thigh bone from drumstick and pull thigh bone up through carcass, out though neck
  8. Put fingers on either side of the breastbone and pull carcass out
  9. Cut any sinews joining carcass to flesh
  10. Truss legs together to make sure the bird cooks evenly
  11. Using a trussing needle, sew up neck
  12. Pan-fry lightly
