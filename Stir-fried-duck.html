Stir-fried duck suprême with egg-fried rice

To make this recipe, we first fried a duck breast, skin-side down and left it to render down the fat, then finished it off in the oven so that the meat was rose and the skin was crispy.

While that was happening, we stir-fried some cold cooked rice and stirred through sesame oil.

We also sliced some water chestnuts and stir-fried them with beansprouts and bamboo shoots.

Finally, we deglazed the pan with oyster sauce and used it to napé the bottom of a serving dish, while in another pan we fried a simple omelette and rolled the rice in it.

When the duck breast was rested and sliced, we served these all together in one dish with the vegetables garnishing on top.
