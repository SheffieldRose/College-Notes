Lasagne al forno

To make this, we layered sheets of lasagne pasta with béchamel and a ragù made by frying garlic, onion, carrot and celery until soft and translucent, then adding in minced beef until browned and adding white wine, chopped tomatoes, tomato purée parsley, bay leaf and breadcrumbs.

We finished this by gratinating parmesan on top, under a salamander grill.
