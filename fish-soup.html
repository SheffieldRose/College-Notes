Soupe de poisson et rouille

We fried off a white mirepoix, 5 garlic cloves and half a fennel, chopped. Into this we added the zest and juice of one orange, 400g chopped tomatoes, 1 tbsp tomato purée, 1 roasted red pepper, a bay leaf, thyme, saffron, shell-on prawns, 1800g of fish fillets, cayenne pepper and fish stock.

We brought this to the boil and simmered until all was appropriately cooked through. Once we’d done this, we liquidised the whole mix and passed, then put back on the boiling plate to keep warm while we made the rouille garnish. This was spooned in at the end.
