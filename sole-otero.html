Sole Otero

Jacket potato baked, filling mashed, seasoned with added chives – skins back in oven to crisp up. Add back together and put sole fillet rolled on top and basted with butter – put this in the oven. Add cream, 1 part gruyere cheese, 1 part parmesan and about 2tsp of English mustard.
