Chicken piccata with pasta fines herbes

To make this, we raised a supreme and sliced it in half lengthways.

This we then paned in parmesan and pan-fried on both sides.

While this was cooking, we cooked off some pasta that we had prepared in advance and stirred through a mix of finsherbes pesto (made from chervil, parsley, tarragon and chive, garlic, olive oil, parmesan) that we had also chopped in advance.

To finish we stirred through some olive oil and placed the piccata on top.
