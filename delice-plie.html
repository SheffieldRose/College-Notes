Délice de plie Nantua

Delice refers to the shape of the fillet as folded over itself in a "C shape".

In this dish, we prepared a fish mousse from salmon, egg yolks and a whole egg, using the egg’s protein to help bind.

Fill fillet with mousse to create desired shape.

Sit delice in a buttered pan with some white wine for your shallow poaching liquid and a small handful of finely diced shallots.

Cover with a cartouche and poach.

To plate, add cray fish, chopped chives and sauce Nantua

Sauce Nantua:

Sweat and pass -

  • Buttered pan
  • Celery
  • Leek
  • Onion
  • White peppercorns
  • Bay leaf
  • Fish bones
  • Squeeze lemon juice
  • Splash white wine

Flambee and add corn starch to stabilise this sauce -

  • Fried matignon
  • Brandy
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Tomato passata
  • Tomato paste – 1tbsp
  • Fried shallots
  • Tomato concasse
  • Brown shrimp
  • Sweet paprika
  • Fish seasoning

Mix the two together and monter au beurre
