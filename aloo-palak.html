Aloo palak

We started by boiling 200g potatoes for this bread until partly cooked and chopping 150g spinach.

We mixed these together with 475g strong flour, 8g salt, 8g caster sugar, 3g paprika, 8g crushed cumin seeds, 8g crushed fennel seeds, 3g turmeric, 30g butter, 20g yeast, 200g water, 150g chopped onion and 75g mango chutney.

We rested for 1 hour then knocked it back and proved for 40 minutes before cooking at 235°c with steam for 30 minutes.
