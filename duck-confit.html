Duck confit

To prepare the previously salted and spiced duck leg for confit-ing, we trimmed the leg bone to neaten up the skin around the meat and expose the bone for a smart and traditional appearance.

We then fried this on a low heat in a sauteuse pan to achieve a golden colour on the meat.

We then covered this in duck fat and put on a higher heat until small bubbles appeared signalling that the oil was up to a good cooking temperature then placed back on a gentler heat to confit the duck over a long time until the duck was very tender and fully cooked through.
