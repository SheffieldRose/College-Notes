Chickpea and aubergine daal

This dish started with braised turmeric rice, which we did by adding turmeric and vegetable stock to a pan of rice and leaving on a low heat for the rice to be absorbed.

We added curried lentils, or daal, which was made with yellow split peas, various spices, vegetable stock and tomato purée, which we also braised and vegetable kebabs made with courgette, pepper aubergine and onion which we marinated in spices, oil and tomato puree, seasoned and then grilled under the salamander.
