Prawn cocktail with Marie Rose sauce

For this, we quickly fried some prawns then we diced them and bound them with Marie Rose sauce that we made by mixing Worcestershire sauce, tomato ketchup, mayonnaise, lemon juice and whipped cream.

We served these garnished with cibles of chive and a sprig of micro-basil.
